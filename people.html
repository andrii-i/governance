Former Project Jupyter Leadership#

Prior to December 2022, Jupyter was led by a Benevolent Dictator for Life (BDFL) and a Steering Council.

Benevolent Dictator for Life (retired December 2022)#

Fernando Perez (@fperez) was the Benevolent Dictator for Life (BDFL). He voluntarily relinquished his role after Jupyter transitioned to a new governance model in December 2022.
